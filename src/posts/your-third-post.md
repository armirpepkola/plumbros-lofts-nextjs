## Introduction: The Power of Light

Natural light is the secret ingredient to a truly spectacular loft conversion. It not only makes the space feel larger but also enhances mood and well-being.

### 1. Strategic Window Placement

Consider the path of the sun. We often recommend a combination of rooflights (like Velux) on the north-facing roof slope for consistent, soft light, and larger dormer windows on the south-facing slope for bright, direct sunlight.
